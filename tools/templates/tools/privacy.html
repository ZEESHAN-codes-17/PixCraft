Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use PixCraft, we may collect:

  • Images you upload for processing
  • Contact information (name, email) when you contact us
  • Usage data (IP address, browser type) for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Processing your images with our tools
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improving our services
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse

3. Image Privacy & Security

Your Images Are Protected
  • Encrypted Storage: All uploaded images are stored with encrypted filenames
  • No Human Access: We do NOT view, browse, or access your images
  • Auto-Deletion: Images are automatically deleted after expiration
  • Temporary Storage: Shareable links expire after your chosen duration
  • Secure Processing: Images are processed automatically without human intervention

4. When We May Access Files

We will ONLY access user files in these exceptional cases:

  • Legal requirement (court order, law enforcement request)
  • Security investigation (suspected illegal content, abuse)
  • Technical troubleshooting (with your explicit permission)

We will never browse, use, or share your images for any other purpose.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Share your images with anyone
  • ❌ Use your images for training or any other purpose
  • ❌ Display your images publicly

6. Data Retention

  • Temporary Images: Deleted automatically after link expiration (1 hour to 1 month)
  • Processed Images: Deleted immediately after download
  • Contact Messages: Retained for customer support purposes

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Ask what data we have about you
  • Opt-out of data collection (by not using the service)
  • Contact us about privacy concerns

8. Security Measures

  • Encrypted file storage
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • File validation and sanitization
  • Secure HTTPS connection (in production)
  • Regular security updates

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management
  • Security (CSRF protection)
  • Rate limiting

We do NOT use tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

PixCraft is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: Contact us through our Contact Form
Our Commitment

At PixCraft, we respect your privacy. Your images are yours alone. We're here to provide tools, not to access your personal content. Your trust is our priority.
